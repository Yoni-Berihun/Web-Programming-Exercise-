A Love's Whisper 

In your eyes, I see the stars, 
A universe where love is ours. 
Each heartbeat sings a sweet refrain,
Together, through joy and pain.

With every touch, the world fades away,
In your embrace, I long to stay. 
Love's gentle whisper, soft and true,
My heart, my soul, forever with you.
